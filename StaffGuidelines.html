Staff Guidelines

Select your staff role and follow the correct procedures.

🛡️

Moderator

Handles moderation reports, warnings and rule enforcement.

🎧

Support

Handles tickets and user support.

⭐

Head Mod / Head Support

Supervises moderators and support members.

Moderator

🛡️ Moderator Guidelines

Moderation Logs

  • Review messages flagged by the moderation bot.
  • The bot will ping staff when a message needs checking.
  • Always check context before taking action.
  • If the message breaks the rules, delete/remove it.

Warnings & Punishments

  • Use /warn for rule violations.
  • Use /timeout for more serious situations.
  • Make sure punishments match the severity.
  • Keep evidence for serious cases.

Permissions

Discord

  • Warn users.
  • Timeout users.
  • Delete messages.

Website

  • No special moderation permissions currently.
  • Cannot delete website messages.
  • Website chat moderation requests must go to Head Mod / Head Support.
  • Head Mod / Head Support forwards serious issues to Admins or Owners.
Moderators cannot kick or ban users.
Support

🎧 Support Guidelines

Tickets

  • Help users with questions, issues and reports.
  • Handle tickets professionally and respectfully.
  • Collect information before escalating issues.
  • Stay focused on helping users.

Moderation Situations

  • If rule breaking happens inside a ticket, contact Head Mod / Head Support.
  • If rule breaking happens in general chat, contact a Moderator.
  • Support does not handle moderation punishments.

Permissions

Discord

  • View tickets.
  • Close tickets.
  • Lock tickets.
  • Unlock tickets.
  • Cannot view closed tickets.

Website

  • View tickets.
  • Close tickets.
  • Lock tickets.
  • Unlock tickets.
  • Cannot view closed tickets.
Supervisor

⭐ Head Mod / Head Support Guidelines

Staff Supervision

  • Watch over Moderators and Support members.
  • Review staff choices and confirm they are correct.
  • Correct staff members if a mistake is made.
  • Guide staff so situations are handled properly.
  • Make sure everyone follows the staff guidelines.

Permissions

Discord

  • All Moderator permissions.
  • All Support permissions.
  • Warn users.
  • Timeout users.
  • Delete messages.
  • Manage tickets.
  • Cannot view closed tickets.

Website

  • View tickets.
  • Close tickets.
  • Lock tickets.
  • Unlock tickets.
  • Warn users.
  • Timeout users.
  • Cannot delete website messages.
  • Cannot view closed tickets.

Escalations

  • Review reports sent by Moderators or Support.
  • Check evidence before escalating.
  • If a kick or ban is needed, contact an Administrator or Owner.
  • Explain the situation and provide proof.
Head Mod / Head Support cannot kick or ban users directly.
Your responsibility is supervising staff, improving decisions and keeping moderation fair.
